Publish content

We offer different platforms for publishing learning content. You may want to publish your content in multiple locations. This section provides criteria to help you select which platform to use.

Available learning platforms

Below is a list of our learning platforms with a link to more information.

    GCcampus       Open learning   GCshare NextGen learning

GCcampus

  • Overview
  • Publishing criteria

GCcampus is a collection of online learning platforms that includes Moodle, Drupal, Kaltura and Saba iLMS. The platforms provide public servants with access to formal training, including courses, videos, and job aids.

    Publish your content on GCcampus if:

  • You require your learners to be tracked as they complete the content
  • You require your learners to have a recording of the content completion on their learner transcripts or they require a certificate of completion
  • Your content is restricted to public servants from departments opted into the School's services, as opposed to being available to everyone, including Canadian citizens
  • Your learning content is part of a certification or learning path
  • You are delivering cohort-based learning
  • Your content is available in the following formats: video (.mp4, Kaltura embed link), Moodle (.mobi), text, images (.jpg, .jpeg or .png), SCORM (version 1.2 zip file) or .h5p 
  • Your content meets our accessibility criteria (WCAG 2.0 AA or higher) (more details here: https://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=2360)
  • Your content meets the official language requirements (more details here: https://www.csps-efpc.gc.ca/termsandconditions/index-eng.aspx#pcol)

Open learning

  • Overview
  • Publishing criteria

Open Learning is the School's open learning platform that includes Moodle and H5P. The platform provides open access to courses, videos, tools and training to the world but does not offer tracking or reporting on learner progress as access is open to all without any registration or login required.

Do not publish to this platform if your primary audience is public servants and you require progress tracking and reporting on learners.

    Publish your content on Open learning if:

  • You DO NOT require your learners be tracked
  • Your learners DO NOT require a record on their transcripts
  • Your content has the permissions and copyright to be made open to everyone, including all Canadian citizens
  • Your content does not require a learner to have an account
  • Your content is available in the following formats: video (.mp4, Kaltura embed link), Moodle (.mobi), text, images (.jpg, .jpeg or .png), SCORM (version 1.2 zip file), .h5p
  • Your content meets our accessibility criteria (WCAG 2.0 AA or higher) (more details here: https://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=2360)
  • Your content meets the official language requirements (more details here: https://www.csps-efpc.gc.ca/termsandconditions/index-eng.aspx#pcol)

GCshare

  • Overview
  • Use case

GCshare provides a space for content creators to upload, download, reuse and share learning content. The platform provides access to open educational resources for content creators.

    Visit GCshare if:

  • You are planning to design or develop new learning content and want to know if something similar may already exist for you to reuse or repurpose
  • You developed content that you think might be useful for other content creators to use

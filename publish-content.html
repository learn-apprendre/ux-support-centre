Publish content

We offer different platforms for publishing your learning content. You may want to publish you content in multiple locations. This section provides criteria to help you select which platform to use.

Below is a list of our learning platforms with a link to more information.

GCcampus Open learning GCshare NextGen learning

Looking for information on how and where to create learning content? Follow the link below.

Create content

GCcampus

GCcampus is a collection of online learning platforms that include Moodle, Drupal, Kaltura and Saba iLMS. The platforms provide public servants with access to formal training including courses, videos, and job aids.

Visit GCcampus site Contact GCcampus team

Publish your content here if:

  • You require your learners to be tracked and recorded on a learner's transcripts.
  • Your content is restricted to public servants from departments opted into the School's services.r.
  • Your learning content is part of a certification or learning path.
  • You are delivering cohort-based learning.
  • Your content is available in the following formats: Kaltura video, Moodle (.mobi), text, images, SCORM or .h5p.

Open learning

Open learning is the School's open learning platform that includes Moodle and H5P. The platform provides access to courses, videos, tools and training to the word.

Visit Open learning site Contact Open learning team

Publish your content here if:

  • You DO NOT require your learners be tracked and recorded on a learner's transcripts.
  • There are no restrictions on who may access your content.
  • Your content does not require a learner to have an account.
  • Your content is available in the following formats: Kaltura video, Moodle (.mobi), text, images, SCORM or .h5p.

GCshare

GCshare provides a space for content creators to upload, download, reuse and share learning content. The platform provides access to open educational resources for content creators.

Visit GCshare website Contact GCshare team

Visit GCshare if:

  • You are planing to design or develop new learning content and want to know if something similar may already exist for you to use or repurpose.
  • You developed learning content that you think might be useful for other content creators to use.

NextGen learning (coming soon!)

The NextGen learning initiative is the replacement of GCcampus with BrightSpace, Desire2Learn (D2L)'s learning management system.

Please note that this initiative is still in the implementation phase.
